Take the reins of radiation-blasted animals known as Critters starring in an eco-apocalyptic Saturday-morning cartoon show in this goofy and exciting tabletop roleplaying game (TTRPG) from Odd Gob Games and the Stillfleet Studio!

Using the innovative Grit System, Blister Critters drops players into a bizarre posthuman world of Blisters (mutations), physics-bending Nonsense powers, dangerous feral Beasts, and a near-infinite amount of human Stuff.

The Blister Critters Rulebook contains 100 pages of mayhem, including player-empowering rules for exploring odd and eerily familiar locations in a vast, cruel suburbia:

  • 6 Critter categories consisting of 66 different animals
  • 4 Blister Paths full of dozens of mutant powers and cartoon-logic Nonsense powers
  • 10 cartoon Roles to play in your party
  • Mind-blowing full-color art by Anthony Grasso (Odd Gob Games)
  • A unique day/night cycle that reflects the fluctuating dangers of the eco-apocalyptic Critterverse
  • A new take on the Grit System, originally built to power the sci-fi game Stillfleet
  • Simple narrative rules for Catchphrases, Ad-Libs, and other cartoon-show fun-all new to the Grit System
  • Dozens and dozens of examples of Stuff and how a creative Critter might employ it
  • Intuitive moves for the Producer (the GM)
  • A full starter episode, "The Gems of Blisterbeak"
  • A bonus Mausritter crossover, "The Critter of a Thousand Blisters"
  • A full-color fillable character sheet as well as a fillable printer-friendly version
