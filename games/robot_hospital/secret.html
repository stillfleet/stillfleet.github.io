Get a free Robot Hospital playmat!

Stack your bots in style

If you are attending a live Stillfleet Studio event, just show this page to a Studio member to receive a free Robot Hospital playmat while supplies last.

This full-color playmat—designed by the game’s creator, Aaron H., and our lead graphic designer, Sunaree Paiwong—helps you dive into the game by showing you where to place each stack of cards. You don’t need it to play, but the official playmat will unlock a new level of stylishness at your table!

Thank you for playing Robot Hospital, and remember: it’s your sworn duty to repair those bots!
