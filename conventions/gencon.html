Preparing to play

What should I bring?

We will have pencils, dice, and source books at the table, but feel free to bring your own!

Will you provide pregenerated characters?

For any given game, we’ll have a broad variety of pregenerated characters at the table.

Am I allowed to bring an existing character?

If you want to bring a character you’ve played before to Gen Con, please bring them! We’ll respect levels they’ve earned in your home games up to 5th level.

I want to make a character before game time—how do I get started?

Use the resources below to make a 1st-level character. If you play more than one game with us, you’ll have opportunities to level up in between sessions, and our GMs are happy to help!

Stillfleet

  1. Print a Stillfleet character sheet
  2. Create a character using either:
    • The Stillfleet Quickstart Rules (PWYW) or
    • The STRINGS : Voidminer Recruitr tool which lets you randomly generate a voidminer in a single click!
  3. Transcribe your character onto the character sheet.

Blister Critters

  • Print a character sheet:
    • Blister Critters Fillable Character Sheet, Color (PDF)
    • Blister Critters Fillable Character Sheet, Black-and-White
  • Pick up the Blister Critters Quickstart Rules (PWYW) and make a Critter.
